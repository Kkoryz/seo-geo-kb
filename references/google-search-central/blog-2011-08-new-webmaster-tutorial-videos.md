# New webmaster tutorial videos | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Over the past couple of years, we've released over 375 videos on our
YouTube channel,
with the majority of them answering direct questions from webmasters. Today, we're starting to
release a freshly baked batch of videos, and you might notice that some of these are a little
different. Don't worry, they still have Matt Cutts in a variety of colored shirts. Instead of
only focusing on quick answers to specific questions, we've created some longer videos which cover
important webmaster-related topics. For example, if you were wondering what the limits are for
