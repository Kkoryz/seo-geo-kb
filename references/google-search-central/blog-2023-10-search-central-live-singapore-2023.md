# Search Central Live Singapore 2023 | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Wednesday, October 18, 2023

We're excited to announce that
[Search Central Live Singapore](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-singapore-2023)
is coming back this year again on November 22. As usual, the event is hosted by the Google Search
team and we're looking forward to bringing together the community to learn, share, and network.

At Search Central Live Singapore we'll cover a
[wide range of topics](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-singapore-2023/agenda)
related to search marketing and resources to help site owners succeed in Search, including:

- what are Google updates,
- how to analyze traffic changes,
- how to deal with international and localized content.

All talks are in English.

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, Search Central Live Singapore has something for everyone. It's the perfect opportunity to get up-to-date insights on the latest trends and connect with other web professionals.

## Registration is now open!

[Registrations for Search Central Live Singapore 2023](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-singapore-2023/forms/registration)
are now open until November 8 and we welcome you to fill out the form for a chance to secure your
spot at the event; that's all you need to do. If you are reading this blog post, use the
registration code `SearchCentral`

when prompted to help us with the selection process.
We have a limited number of seats available and we will let you know if your spot is confirmed by
November 15.

We look forward to seeing you there!
