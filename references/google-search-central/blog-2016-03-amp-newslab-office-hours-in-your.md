# AMP NewsLab Office Hours in your language | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Wednesday, March 02, 2016

[Accelerated Mobile Pages](https://www.ampproject.org/) (AMP) is a
global, industry-wide initiative, with publishers large and small all focused on the same goal: a
better, faster mobile web.

We've had a great response to our English language
[AMP office hours](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLXYuDvosdAxxy-KyxWsrXPs6Gt2HAYoQv),
but we know that English isn't everyone's native language.

For the next two weeks, we're rolling out a new series of office hours in French, Italian, German, Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, and Indonesian and invite everyone to learn about AMP in their native language. Product Managers, Technical Managers, and Engineers at Google, will get to speak in their native tongue, and answer any questions you may have on AMP.

First we will reintroduce you to AMP and how it works, before diving into the technical specs and
various components of AMP. You can add your questions via the Q and A app on the event pages
below, and we will answer them during the office hours. You can also watch them on the News Lab
[YouTube page](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC6CSDSDMpceGOr_9N0W3kUg)
after the event.
