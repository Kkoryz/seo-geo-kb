# Search Central Live Zurich is back! | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Friday, September 29, 2023

We're very excited to announce that Search Central Live is coming back to Zurich on Tuesday, October 24th, 2023! After our last Search Central Live event in Zurich in December 2019, we're back in town for more presentations, Q&A and networking!

The event will have a few presentations from Google Search teams, including the opening keynote by John Mueller and a Q&A with members of the Google Search Relations team. The event will be held in English at the Google offices in Zurich.

Meet the local SEO community, web developers, website owners and publishers and engage with folks from Google Search teams. In between presentations, you'll have the opportunity to meet old and new friends, exchange ideas and ask questions!

Be there or be square - no recordings or live stream will be available afterwards, so everyone can be as open as they want to be and engage with one another.

The event will take place on October 24th, between 14:00 and 19:00, in the Google offices in Zurich, Switzerland. Check more details about speakers, agenda, and more, in the event website. Unfortunately, we can't accommodate everyone. We'll send invitations by October 10 to accepted attendees, you'll need that email to enter the event.

We're excited to welcome you back to the Google Zurich office and talk about all things Google Search!
