# Expanding support for APAC Search Central Help Communities | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

As we’ve been continuously working to improve support for website owners in more language markets,
we’re very excited to announce the opening of our Chinese,
Korean,
Vietnamese,
and Thai
Search Central Help Communities with support from a global team of community managers that are
dedicated to helping Product
Experts.

For current Search Central Product Experts, we will honor your badge level in the newly opened APAC
communities. If you previously contributed and would like to start contributing again, you can
start posting in the forum. Also, do reach out to your friends who have contributed in the
past.
