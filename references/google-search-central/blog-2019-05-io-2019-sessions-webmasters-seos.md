# Google I/O 2019 - What sessions should SEOs and webmasters watch? | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Monday, May 06, 2019

[Google I/O 2019](https://events.google.com/io/)
is starting tomorrow and will run for 3 days, until Thursday. Google I/O is our yearly developers
festival, where product announcements are made, new APIs and frameworks are introduced, and
Product Managers present the latest from Google to an audience of 7,000+ developers who fly to
California.

However, you don't have to physically attend the event to take advantage of this once-a-year
opportunity: many conferences and talks are live streamed on YouTube for anyone to watch. Browse
the
[full schedule of events](https://events.google.com/io/schedule),
including a list of talks that we think will be interesting for webmasters to watch (all talks are
in English). All the links shared below will bring you to pages with more details about each talk,
and links to watch the sessions will display on the day of each event. All times are Pacific
Central time **(California time)**.

-
__Tuesday, May 7th__**4pm**-[Building Successful Websites: Case Studies for Mature and Emerging Markets](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/50bbf64a-1cc4-4169-b91b-1b124b769d07), with Aancha Bahadur, Charlie Croom, Matt Doyle, Rudra Kasturi, and Jesar Shah -
__Wednesday, May 8th__**10.30am**-[Enhance Your Search and Assistant Presence with Structured Data](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/40a400cf-aa8d-4510-8491-07d1e0248f06), with Aylin Altiok and Will Leszczuk**11.30am**-[Create App-like Experiences on Google Search and the Google Assistant](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/1bbd42a2-3ba3-4b99-88da-48474fc3bb91), with Allen Harvey**11.30am**-[Rapidly Building Better Web Experiences with AMP](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/5311d3a8-03b4-4ae7-ba22-a3990ef1913a), with Adam Greenberg and Naina Raisinghani**6.30pm**-[Unlocking New Capabilities for the Web](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/7e886111-34a3-4920-ae73-7e13e5bafc0f), with Pete LePage and Thomas Steiner -
__Thursday, May 9th__**10.30am**-[Google Search: State of the Union](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/280ec615-7a63-420a-9e74-05428da072f9), with John Mueller and Martin Splitt**1.30pm**-[Google Search and JavaScript Sites](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/da7ca544-1260-4940-88c6-e74dbf49a80a), with Zoe Clifford and Martin Splitt

This list is only a small part of the agenda that we think is useful to webmasters and SEOs. There
are many more sessions that you could find interesting! To learn about those other talks, check
out the
[full list of "web" sessions](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/?2=topic_web),
[design sessions](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/?2=topic_design),
[Cloud sessions](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/?2=topic_cloud),
[machine learning sessions](https://events.google.com/io/schedule/events/?2=topic_mlai),
and more. Use the filtering function to toggle the sessions on and off.

We hope you can make the time to watch the talks online, and participate in the excitement of I/O! The videos will also be available on Youtube after the event, in case you can't tune in live.
