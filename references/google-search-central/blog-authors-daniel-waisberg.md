Daniel Waisberg

Search Advocate

Daniel is a Search Advocate at Google, part of the Search Relations team. His job is
divided between educating the Search community and working with the Search Console engineering team
to develop new product capabilities. In particular, he specializes in deep dives into how to use
data to do cool things and making sure that no one uses a pie chart.

He also leads specific developer
focused features on Search Console, such as the URL Inspection API
and the Bulk data export. Check out his posts on the
Google Search Central Blog.

In addition to blog posts, Daniel invests a lot of his time producing videos for the Search
Central Youtube channel. You may recognize him from his
Search Console training series, where he focuses on onboarding everyone to Search Console, in video format.
Check out videos from Daniel Waisberg on the Google Search Central Youtube channel.
