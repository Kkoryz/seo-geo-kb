# Las Vegas Pubcon 2006 | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Monday, November 13, 2006

As if working at Google isn't already a party, today I'm traveling to Las Vegas for
[WebmasterWorld PubCon 2006](https://www.pubcon.com/)! But instead
of talking bets and odds, I'll be talking about how Google can help webmasters improve their
sites. I love chatting with webmasters about all the work that goes into creating a great website.
Several other Googlers will be there too, so if you have a burning question or just wanna talk
about random stuff you can stop us and say hi. Besides the sessions, we'll be at the Google booth
on Wednesday and Thursday, so come by and introduce yourself.
