# Webmaster Central YouTube update for May 26-29 | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

In February, we launched the
Webmaster Central YouTube channel,
and since then we've been busy keeping it fresh with new content. We're not yet uploading
20 hours every minute,
but did you know that we've been releasing a new video almost every weekday? You can keep up
with the latest updates by subscribing to our channel in YouTube, or by adding
our feed
to your RSS reader.

If you're more of a weekly digest kind of person, we're here for you too. For every week that we
have new videos available, we'll let you know here on the blog. In the videos uploaded in the past
week, you'll find answers to these questions from
