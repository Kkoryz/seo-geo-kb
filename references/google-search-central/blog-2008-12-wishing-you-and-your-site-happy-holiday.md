# Wishing you and your site a happy holiday! | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Your presence is our favorite present—thanks for joining us for another year of making your
site, and therefore the web, a better place.

Every day we see new people commenting and joining the discussion. This holiday season we'll try
to update our blog to accommodate your growing needs. Always you can let us know how we're doing
(especially if we publish a typo! :), because first and foremost and
everywhere in the middle, we're trying to improve for you.

Happy holidays from all of us at Webmaster Central.

Written by Maile Ohye, Developer Programs Tech Lead
