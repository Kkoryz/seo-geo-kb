# Search Central Live is going to Madrid | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Wednesday, March 5, 2025

We're very excited to announce that Search Central Live is going to Madrid for the first time on April 9! Following successful events throughout the world, we're continuing our mission to help you enhance your site's performance in Google Search.


The event will have a mix of presenters from the Google Search, News, and Partnerships teams. The content will be delivered in English and Spanish, but we'll have live translation, so you can listen to all presentations in Spanish if you prefer:

- SEO: what are the best practices to succeed in Google Search?
- Search Console & Google Trends: how to use Search data to make better decisions?
- Google News: which tools are available and how to use them?

If these topics sound interesting to you, whether you're an SEO enthusiast, a website owner, or a publisher, don't miss your chance to participate. Apart from the presentations, you'll have lots of time to chat with the Google Search and News teams, and meet new people in real life. The event won't be recorded or live streamed, that's why we call it live!

The event will take place on April 9, between 9:00 and 14:00, at the Google Campus in Madrid (the
exact address will follow with the invitation). Check more details about speakers, agenda, and more,
in [the event website](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-madrid-2025).

Unfortunately, we can't accommodate everyone. **We'll send invitations on March 20 to accepted
attendees, you'll need that email to enter the event. **

We're looking forward to meeting in person and chatting about Google Search and News.
