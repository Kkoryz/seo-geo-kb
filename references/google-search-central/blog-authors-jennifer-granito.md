Jennifer Granito

Group Product Manager for News Quality

Jennifer Granito is a Group Product Manager for News Quality at Google. She is currently the product lead for
news quality and trustworthiness across Search, the Google News App, and other services at Google.
She is working to help everyone understand the world through access to high quality news content.

She previously worked at a startup, Kifi, which was acquired by Google and focused on connecting people with
knowledgable content to better understand, utilize, and expand what we know together. In addition to
Kifi, she enjoyed her time working at LinkedIn, Zynga, Tagged, MySpace, Hangtime, and AOL Instant
Messenger.

She graduated from James Madison University and has over 15 years of experience in the tech
industry. She is passionate about using technology to make people's lives better and mentors many
young product manager and engineers.
