# We're on LinkedIn (finally) | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

The Google Search Central LinkedIn channel is (finally) here!

If you're a business owner, SEO professional, or anyone who works on a website, you can now
join us on LinkedIn
to stay connected with what's happening with Google Search. We'll delve into updates on (official)
algorithm changes, SEO best practices, tips with using Search Console, and new features on Google
Search.

We're looking forward to joining (connecting with?) you there! If you have any thoughts, you can
let us know on LinkedIn.
