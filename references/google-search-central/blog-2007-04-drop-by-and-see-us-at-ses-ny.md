# Drop by and see us at SES NY | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Friday, April 06, 2007

If you're planning to attend the
[Search Engine Strategies conference](https://www.searchenginestrategies.com/sew/ny07/index)
next week in New York, be sure to come by and say hi! A whole bunch of us from the
[Webmaster Central](/search)
team will be there, looking to talk to you, get your feedback, and answer your questions. Be sure
to join us for lunch on Tuesday, April 10th, where we'll spend an hour answering any question
you may have. And then come by our other sessions, or find us in the expo hall or the bar.
