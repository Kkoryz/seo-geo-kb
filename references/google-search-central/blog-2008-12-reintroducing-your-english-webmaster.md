# Reintroducing your English Webmaster Help Google Guides | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Monday, December 08, 2008

When we announced our latest round of
[Bionic Posters in the old Help Group](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/70dd4686b29a4df3/980a583b02067118#980a583b02067118),
an
[astute webmaster noted](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/70dd4686b29a4df3/9b0baf9d8acef765#9b0baf9d8acef765)
that we had never fully introduced ourselves to the webmaster community. With our
[announcement](/search/blog/2008/12/new-look-for-our-webmaster-help-group) of a new
[English Webmaster Help forum](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community),
I'm happy to (re)introduce you all to the Google Guides from around the world who will be hanging
out in the English forum:
