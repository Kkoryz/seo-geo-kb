# Google Webmaster Help Forums in more languages | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Thursday, April 30, 2009

Traditionally when we launch a new communication channel, we also give the shareholders a chance
to introduce themselves. We did so when we opened
[webmaster help communities for European webmasters](/search/blog/2007/11/dozen-ways-to-discuss-webmaster-help)
almost two years ago, and also more than a year ago, when we were able to expand and
[add groups in three more languages](/search/blog/2008/01/growing-webmaster-help-groups-team).
Last December we were very happy to announce the
[re-launch of two of our Help Forums](/search/blog/2008/12/new-look-for-our-webmaster-help-group)
in a new and cool look and feel.

Today, we're happy to announce that we keep on increasing the global dialogue with webmasters,
opening an
[Arabic](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=ar)
and a
[Czech/Slovak](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=cs)
Webmaster Help Forum. Furthermore, we would like to highlight the support we offer in
[Chinese](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=zh-CN),
[Japanese](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=ja), and
[Korean](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=ko).
While we've offered support to Chinese webmasters for a little more than a year, the Japanese and
Korean forums are only a few weeks old. Keeping with tradition, the guides monitoring our new
forums would like to introduce themselves to the global webmaster family:
