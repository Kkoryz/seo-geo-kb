# Showcase your site's reviews in Search | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Wednesday, September 07, 2016

Today, we're introducing Reviews from the web to local Knowledge Panels, to accompany our
[recently launched](https://search.googleblog.com/2016/08/never-miss-must-try-with-google-search.html)
best-of lists and critic reviews features. Whether your site publishes editorial critic reviews,
best-of places lists, or aggregates user ratings, this content can be featured in local Knowledge
Panels when users are looking for places to go.

## Reviews from the web

Available globally on mobile and desktop, Reviews from the web brings aggregated user ratings of up to three review sites to Knowledge Panels for local places across many verticals including shops, restaurants, parks and more.

By implementing
[review snippet markup](/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/review-snippet#type_definitions)
and meeting
[our criteria](/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/review-snippet#review-snippet-guidelines),
your site's user-generated composite ratings will be eligible for inclusion. Add the
[Local Business markup](/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/review-snippet#review-subject-properties)
to help Google match reviews to the right review subject and help grow your site's coverage. For
more information on the guidelines for the Reviews from the web, critic review and top places
lists features, check out our [developer site](/search).

## Critic reviews

In the U.S. on mobile and desktop, qualifying publishers can participate in the
[critic review feature](/search/blog/2016/08/promote-your-local-businesses-reviews)
in local Knowledge Panels. Critic reviews possess an editorial tone of voice and have an
opinionated position on the local business, coming from an editor or on-the-ground expert. For
more information on how to participate, see the details in our
[critic reviews page](/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/review-snippet#critic-reviews).

The local information across Google Search helps millions of people, every day, discover and share
great places. If you have any questions, please visit our
[webmaster forums](https://support.google.com/webmasters/go/community).
