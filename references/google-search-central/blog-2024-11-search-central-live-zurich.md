# Join us at Search Central Live in Zurich! | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

We invite you to join us for Search Central Live Zurich 2024 on Dec 12, 2024 in the Google office in Zurich!
We are looking forward to meeting you for a selection of presentations from the teams behind Google Search.
Like last year, we want to give an opportunity to a few of you to present a 15 minute lightning talk and share what you've learned with others from the ecosystem and the local communities.
If you're interested in becoming a community presenter, please fill out this form.

As we have a limited number of seats for this event, we invite you to apply for a free ticket to participate and ask you to only apply if you plan on attending in person in Zurich.

Our application form is open until December 4th and we'll get back to you latest by December 8.

With the Search Central Live events we occasionally run around the world we want to connect with different communities to learn more about the specific challenges and opportunities these communities deal with, so we hope to see many of you from around Europe and especially the DACH region. If you're interested in these events but are a long way away from Zurich, keep an eye on the event calendar to hear about the next opportunities in your area.
Hope to see you soon in Zurich!
