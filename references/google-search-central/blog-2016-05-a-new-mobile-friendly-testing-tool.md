# The Search Console mobile friendly testing tool (retired) | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

The Search Console mobile friendly testing tool (retired)

Tuesday, May 17, 2016

Mobile is close to our heart—we love seeing more and more sites make their content available
in useful and accessible ways for mobile users. To help keep the ball rolling, we've now launched
a new Mobile Friendly Test.

The updated tool provides us with room to continue to improve on its functionality, and over time,
we expect it to replace the previous Mobile Friendly Test. Additionally, of course this tool also
works well on your smartphone, if you need to double-check something there!

We'd like to invite you to take it for a spin, try your website and other sites that you're
curious about! Let us know how you like it—either here in the comments or in our
webmaster help forums.
