# Using Webmaster Tools like an SEO | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

We on the Webmaster Central team aren't SEOs, but that doesn't stop me from
pretending to be one! In our latest video, I'll talk about utilizing some features in
Webmaster Tools as
though I were the SEO for www.googlestore.com.

Just as a grandparent raves about their grandchild, I could have gone on for hours about
(my baby!) Webmaster Tools. Thankfully I stopped myself—but if you have tips to share or
questions to ask, please post in our
forum.

Maile Ohye,
