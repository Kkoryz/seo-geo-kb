We published episode 2 of our webtoon series in Korean. Here's a summary of the episode in English:

Cherry logs in to Search Console and sees a lot of 404 errors, as well as warning and exclusion messages in the Indexing Coverage report. She is excited to learn about the queries that led the band's fans to her site, but also realizes one of her biggest mistakes: the band's schedule has been uploaded as an image file. While Cherry and Eco Bag discuss various aspects of analytics in the Performance report, and making it easier for search engines to understand a website, Cherry's father Choco Muffin stops by and asks for help to promote the Small Business Association. Cherry recommends that her father uses Search Console at the start of building his website.
