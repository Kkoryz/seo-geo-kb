# An update on the Webmaster Central Blog | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

We've got a new URL! You may have noticed the Google Webmaster Central blog has a new address:
webmasters.googleblog.com.

That's because starting today, Google is moving its blogs to a new domain to help people recognize
when they're reading an official blog from Google. These changes will roll out to all of Google's
blogs over time.

The previous address will redirect to the new domain, so your bookmarks and links will continue
to work. Unfortunately, as with a custom domain change in Blogger, the
Google+ comments on the blogs have been reset.
