# Webmaster Tools now in 26 languages | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Wednesday, May 14, 2008

Webmasters come from all corners of the world and we are working hard to reach each and everyone
of you. A few months back we introduced you to
[Googlers](/search/blog/2008/01/growing-webmaster-help-groups-team)
who help monitor our Webmaster Help Groups in fifteen languages. Since then, that number has
grown to sixteen with the addition of the
[Chinese Help Group](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=zh-CN).

Today, we're happy to announce that
[Webmaster Tools](https://search.google.com/search-console)
is now available in four more languages:

- Arabic
- Hebrew
- Hindi
- Thai

Webmaster Tools is already available in 22 other languages: British English, Czech, Danish, Dutch, Finnish, French, German, Hungarian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Simplified Chinese, Spanish, Swedish, Traditional Chinese, Turkish, and US English.

We're thrilled to be engaging with such a diverse community of webmasters. Thank you again for your feedback and support!
