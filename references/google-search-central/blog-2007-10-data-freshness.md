# Data freshness | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Common feedback we hear from webmasters is that you want us to improve the freshness of the data
in Webmaster Tools. Understood. :) We've increased the update
frequency for your
verified sites'
data, such as
crawl,
index,
and
search query
stats. Much of this data depends on the content of your site. If your content doesn't change very
often, or if you're not getting new links to your site, you may not see updates to your data
every time you sign in to Webmaster Tools.
