# Search Central Live is back to Mexico City | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Wednesday, August 20, 2025

We're very excited to announce that Search Central Live is coming back to Ciudad de Mexico on September 25! Following successful events throughout the world earlier this year, we're continuing our mission to help you enhance your site's performance in Google Search.

The event will have a mix of presenters from the Google Search, News, and Partnerships teams. The content will be delivered in English and Spanish, but we'll have live translation, so you can listen to all presentations in Spanish if you prefer.

Whether you're an SEO enthusiast, a website owner, or a publisher, don't miss your chance to participate. In addition to the presentations, you'll have lots of time to chat with the Google Search and News teams, and meet new people in real life. As this is a live event with limited access, we encourage you to prioritize registering your attendance.

The event will take place on September 25, between 8:30 and 16:00, in the Colonia Roma neighborhood of Mexico City.
Check more details about speakers, agenda, and more, in the [event website](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-mexico-2025).
Registration will be open until August 29th, and confirmations will be sent on September 4th, including the
address and other event details.

We're looking forward to meeting in person and chatting about Google Search and News.
