# Announcing Search Central Live Argentina | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Thursday, February 1, 2024

We're excited to announce that Search Central Live is coming to Buenos Aires on March 5, 2024. Following successful events throughout the world last year, we're continuing our mission to help website owners to enhance their site's performance in Google Search.


The event will have a mix of presenters from the Google Search, News, and Partnerships teams; we'll also have a special participation from
[Aleyda Solis](https://www.aleydasolis.com/). The content will be delivered in English and Spanish, and we'll have live translation,
so you can listen to all presentations in Spanish if you prefer. Here are a few of the topics we'll discuss:

- SEO best practices: making sure your website can be crawled, indexed and served on Google
- Search Console: using data to succeed in Google Search
- Google News Argentina: partnering with news sites to help them prosper in Search

If the topics above sound interesting to you, whether you're an SEO enthusiast, a website owner, or a publisher, don't miss your chance to participate.

Apart from the presentations, you'll have lots of time to chat with the Google Search and News teams, and meet new people in real life. The event will not be recorded or live streamed, that's why we call it live!

Check more details about speakers, agenda, and more, on the [event website](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-argentina-2024).
Unfortunately, we can't accommodate everyone. We'll send invitations on February 19 to accepted attendees, you'll need that email to enter the event.

We're looking forward to meeting in person and chatting about Google Search and News.
