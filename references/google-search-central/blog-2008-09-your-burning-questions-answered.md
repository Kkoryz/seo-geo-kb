# Your burning questions - Answered! | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Wednesday, September 10, 2008

In a recent [blog post](/search/blog/2008/08/impact-of-user-feedback-part-2-and-more)
highlighting our
[Webmaster Help Group](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community),
I asked for your webmaster-related questions. In our second installment of
[Popular Picks](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/e213eec10610a481),
we hoped to discover which issues webmasters wanted to learn more about, and then respond with
some better documentation on those topics. It looks like it was a success, so please get clicking:

-
**Image Search ranking:**Starting things off,[JimAtArtsnovaDotCom asked](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/e213eec10610a481/#c73d8d6d53850aeb)about how Google indexes images. Evan details the factors that account for a[site's images appearing in Google's image search](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/e37227930654391e). -
**Sitelinks and Webmaster Tools:**Maile tackled[a request from Red Cardinal](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/e213eec10610a481/#f968695a7cac467d)by providing a more detailed[explanation of sitelinks](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Tools/browse_thread/thread/63b915eed3386a60), particularly what happens when they are blocked by a user. -
**Reconsideration Requests:**Want to learn more about reconsideration requests? In response to[a message from Chibcha](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/e213eec10610a481/#369f15dbd44b1a2d), Susan provides additional context on[when you should file a reconsideration request](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/97db1e0a05f4357d)and other tips to address your site's performance. -
**Redirects:**In response to[Cristina's question](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/e213eec10610a481/#663ff183c887de3e)about site moves and redirects, John explains how[Googlebot handles absolute and relative URLs in your redirect to a new domain](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/ebffe06e5399d192). -
**Communication with webmasters:**Finally, I responded to[a message from bluegill01](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/e213eec10610a481/#16ce6f63f424a065), noting the scenarios when you can expect to[receive a message from Google](https://groups.google.com/group/Google_Webmaster_Help-Indexing/browse_thread/thread/6fa677629a9e2a70).

Thanks again for your questions! See you around the group.
