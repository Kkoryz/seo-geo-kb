Aurora Morales

Trust & Safety

Aurora works on the Google Trust & Safety team. For many years she has been
dedicated to educating the industry on product policies and guidelines to help create a safer
ecosystem for diverse audiences.

Some of the projects that have kept her busy: managing the English and Spanish speaking Search
Central Help Communities; educating publisher audiences on Google's monetization policies and
restrictions; engaging key opinion formers on risk and abuse areas, such as misinformation.
