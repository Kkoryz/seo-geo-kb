# Trouble with verification | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Some of you have had one of the following responses when trying to
verify:

"Our system has experienced a temporary problem."

or,

"The system is currently busy. Please try again in a few minutes."

However, when you do try again later, you continue to get one of these messages. This is a known
issue and we are working to resolve it as quickly as possible. Thanks for your patience.
