# The Webmaster Academy goes international | Google Search Central Blog | Google for Developers

Since we launched the Webmaster Academy in English
back in May 2012, its educational
content has been viewed well over 1 million times.

The
Webmaster Academy
was built to guide webmasters in creating great sites that perform well in Google search results.
It is an ideal guide for beginner webmasters but also a recommended read for experienced users who
wish to learn more about advanced topics.

To support webmasters across the globe, we're happy to announce that we're launching the Webmaster
Academy in 20 languages. So whether you speak Japanese or Italian, we hope we can help you to make
even better websites! You can easily access it through Webmaster Central.

We'd love to read your comments here and invite you to join the discussion in the
help forums.
