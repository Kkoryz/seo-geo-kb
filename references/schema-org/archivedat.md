# archivedAt

A Schema.org Property

*This term is in the "new" area - implementation feedback and adoption from applications and websites can help improve our definitions.*

- Canonical URL: https://schema.org/archivedAt
[Check for open issues.](https://github.com/schemaorg/schemaorg/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+archivedAt)

Indicates a page or other link involved in archival of a

[CreativeWork](/CreativeWork). In the case of[MediaReview](/MediaReview), the items in a[MediaReviewItem](/MediaReviewItem)may often become inaccessible, but be archived by archival, journalistic, activist, or law enforcement organizations. In such cases, the referenced page may not directly publish the content.### Used on these types
