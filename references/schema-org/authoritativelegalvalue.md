# AuthoritativeLegalValue

A Schema.org Enumeration Member

*This term is in the "new" area - implementation feedback and adoption from applications and websites can help improve our definitions.*

- Canonical URL: https://schema.org/AuthoritativeLegalValue
[Check for open issues.](https://github.com/schemaorg/schemaorg/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+AuthoritativeLegalValue)

Indicates that the publisher gives some special status to the publication of the document. ("The Queens Printer" version of a UK Act of Parliament, or the PDF version of a Directive published by the EU Office of Publications). Something "Authoritative" is considered to be also

[OfficialLegalValue](/OfficialLegalValue)".A member value for enumeration type:

[LegalValueLevel](/LegalValueLevel)
