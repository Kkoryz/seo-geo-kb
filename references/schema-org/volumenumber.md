# volumeNumber

A Schema.org Property

- Canonical URL: https://schema.org/volumeNumber
- Equivalent Property: bibo:volume
[Check for open issues.](https://github.com/schemaorg/schemaorg/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+volumeNumber)

Identifies the volume of publication or multi-part work; for example, "iii" or "2".
