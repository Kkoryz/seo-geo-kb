# Competitors: Overview

Add a top 1% marketer to your team

Module 8

In this report, we show how your keyword rankings compare with up to ten competing websites.

Below are some actionable ways to use this report.

Click on any of your competitors’ names to sort the table by their domain.

This will show you keywords where you’re ranking below your competitors. This is a great way to find pages to prioritize your SEO efforts.

Hit the "Position History" dropdown to see a graph of your competitors' ranking progress versus yours.
