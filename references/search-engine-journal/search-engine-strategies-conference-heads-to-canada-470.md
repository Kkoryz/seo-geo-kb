# Search Engine Strategies Conference Heads to Canada

Jupitermedia Corporation today announced that its inaugural Search Engine Strategies Conference & Expo 2004, will be held May 11 – 12 at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre, Toronto, Canada. In 2004, Search Engine Strategies will hold events in New York, Tokyo, London, San Jose, Stockholm and Chicago.

Organized by search authority Chris Sherman, Associate Editor, SearchEngineWatch.com and President, Searchwise, this two-day event covers issues specific to the Canadian marketplace. Search Engine Strategies delivers real-time actionable information needed to grow a business through search engine marketing; teaches the ins-and-outs of search engine marketing from top experts and search engines themselves (both Canadian-based and from around the globe) and provides a unique setting to network with fellow marketers.

Confirmed speakers from Alchemist Media Inc., AOL, Ask Jeeves, Bizresearch, Grantastic Designs Inc., Google, IBM, Mamma.com, MSN.ca, Overture, Shopping.com and Singingfish, among others, will be in attendance.

Premier Plus Sponsor for Search Engine Strategies Conference & Expo 2004 is Google. Premier Business Sponsor is Sympatico/MSN. Premier Sponsors include GenieKnows.com, Kanoodle and Mamma.com. Analyst Sponsor for the event is JupiterResearch. Additional sponsors & exhibitors include Advertising.com, ClickTracks, Enhance Interactive, Hot Banana, iCrossing, iLOR, IMPAQT, Meandaur Interactive, NetWorldMedia, Overture, Performics, The Red Pages, RevQuest.com, Search Engine Positioning, WorldChoiceTravel.com and Worldwide Broadcast Network. Search Engine Strategies Conference & Expo 2004 is hosted by SearchEngineWatch.com, SEMList.com and internet.com, and is produced by Jupitermedia Corporation.
