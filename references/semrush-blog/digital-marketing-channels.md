# 10 Effective Digital Marketing Channels & How to Use Them

This article highlights 10 digital marketing channels and explains how to use each channel to drive more business.

We’ve used these channels and tactics to bring more than 35M visitors per month to Semrush.com ([Traffic Analytics](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/traffic/) data).

Before diving in, here are the basics.

## What Are Digital Marketing Channels?

Digital marketing channels—also known as digital advertising channels—are online platforms and methods businesses use to promote products, services, or brands.

Examples include websites, social media, email, SEO, PPC, and content marketing. They allow businesses to reach and engage with their audience in digital spaces.

Below are 10 effective digital marketing channels.

## 10 Effective Digital Marketing Channels for 2025

### 1. Website

A website is the online representation of a business or brand. It showcases products, services, values, and personality to potential customers.

For example, Semrush’s website homepage represents the Semrush brand and explains what users can do with a subscription.

Websites can:

-
Build credibility and authority in a niche

-
Increase visibility and reach

-
Provide a platform to communicate with an audience

-
Collect data and feedback from visitors

-
Allow versatile marketing campaigns


#### How to Get Started with Your Website

##### 1. Understand Your Audience

Research their demographics, preferences, pain points, and goals. [One2Target](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/one2target/) can help gather these insights—just enter your domain and view data about your visitors.

##### 2. Design Your Website with Your Audience in Mind

Hosting and website-building services like [Wix](http://wix.com) and [WordPress](http://wordpress.com) offer ready-made templates. Customize one to suit your audience and optimize it for your unique style. Focus on:

-
**Structure**: Organize content to make navigation easy -
**Content**: Provide valuable information that addresses visitors’ questions and solves their problems -
**Style**: Maintain a consistent visual identity that reflects brand personality and values

##### 3. Test and Optimize Your Site

Track performance with [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/) or the [free SEO Checker](https://www.semrush.com/siteaudit/) from Semrush. Make incremental changes based on collected data to better serve your audience.

### 2. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

[SEO](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-is-seo/) is the process of improving a site’s visibility in search engines. It generates unpaid traffic, leads, and sales.

SEO is effective because it targets users who are actively searching for products or services.

For example, investment firm Charles Schwab created a landing page optimized for “Roth IRA.”

It includes secondary keywords like “what is a Roth IRA” and “why consider a Roth IRA.”

That page ranks number one on Google with a featured snippet.

It receives around 327K organic visitors per month ([Organic Rankings](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/organic/) data).

### 7. Video Marketing

Video marketing uses video content to promote brands, products, or services. Video marketing is effective because it engages multiple senses and can convey information more effectively than text.

For example, DJI publishes product introductions on YouTube and receives thousands to over a million views per video.

#### How to Start Reaching Customers with Video Marketing

Start by setting goals and defining your audience. Decide your video’s purpose and the people you want to reach.

Next, choose the right video type. Explainers, testimonials, live streams, and sponsored videos are all popular formats.

Then, create and share videos on key platforms. Consider YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok, depending on your audience.

Finally, measure performance and optimize. Track views, engagement, and ROI to improve strategy.

Use these tips to make your video marketing more effective:

-
Hook your audience in the first few seconds with a catchy title, thumbnail, and introduction

-
Tell stories that connect with your audience and showcase your personality, values, and benefits

-
Evoke positive emotions and inspire action

-
Make your videos “sticky” by adding elements of surprise, curiosity, or humor

-
Rely on visuals and soundtracks to enhance your message and appeal to different senses

-
Keep your videos concise, clear, and relevant to your goal and audience

Referral marketing:

-

Converts satisfied customers into ambassadors

-
Lowers customer acquisition costs


#### How to Get Started with Referral Marketing

-
**Identify your best customers and advocates.**Use surveys, reviews, or social media to find enthusiastic users. -

**Promote your program.**Make it visible on your website, in emails, and on social media. -
**Monitor and improve.**Track referrals, conversions, and ROI to refine the program.

## Choosing the Right Digital Marketing Channels for Your Business

You don’t need to use every possible channel. Instead, focus on the ones that align with your business, audience, and budget. Test different channels, measure results, and prioritize what works.

Consider these three main factors when choosing your digital marketing channels:

### 1. Business Type

Different business models require different channels.

Physical stores often benefit from local SEO, which includes creating a[ Google Business Profile](https://www.semrush.com/blog/google-my-business/) and listings in online directories.

Ecommerce stores may see more success with social ads, email marketing, and influencer marketing.

### 2. Target Audience

Understand who you want to reach.

Younger, tech-oriented audiences often prefer Instagram and TikTok. Professionals and older users might respond well to LinkedIn or newsletters.

### 3. Budget

Your budget is how much money you can spend on your digital marketing campaigns.

Some channels, like PPC and social ads, can be more expensive but produce quick, trackable results. Others, like SEO and content marketing, are more affordable but require more time to implement.

## Diversify Your Digital Presence and Reach Your Target Audience

These 10 digital marketing channels can help you grow your business. Choose channels that fit your goals, audience, and budget. Then, test each option. Continue investing in the channels that work, and drop those that don’t.

This approach helps you diversify your digital presence and connect with customers wherever they are. Over time, this builds trust, loyalty, and long-term relationships.
