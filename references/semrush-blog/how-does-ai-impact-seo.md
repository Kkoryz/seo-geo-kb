# How does AI impact SEO?

AI impacts [SEO](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-is-seo/) by changing how search engines surface and rank content while providing new tools to optimize your website for both traditional search and AI-powered features.

Google's [AI Overview](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-overviews/) and similar features now answer simple questions directly in search results, which means fewer clicks for basic "what is" or "how to" content. However, AI also drives more traffic to authoritative sources that provide unique insights and comprehensive coverage.

Here's how AI is reshaping SEO:

**Demand higher content quality standards.**AI prioritizes original insights, first-hand experience, and unique data over thin rewrites or generic information that's already widely available**Increase importance of structured data.**Clear formatting,[schema markup](https://www.semrush.com/blog/schema-markup/), and scannable content help AI systems extract and cite your information in AI Overviews and chatbot responses**Shift user behavior toward complex queries.**People ask longer, more conversational questions when using AI search, so you need to optimize for natural language rather than just short keywords**Reward comprehensive topic coverage.**Instead of targeting single[keywords](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-are-keywords/), create content clusters that thoroughly cover related subtopics and user questions**Emphasize expertise and authority signals.**AI systems favor content from recognized experts with clear credentials, citations, and demonstrated experience in their field

Semrush's [Enterprise AIO](https://www.semrush.com/lp/enterprise-aio/) and [AI Visibility Toolkit](https://www.semrush.com/semrush-ai-toolkit/) can help you track how AI platforms represent your brand and monitor your visibility in AI-generated search results compared to competitors.

The key is evolving from basic information provider to trusted expert whose insights AI systems want to reference and users want to read in full.
