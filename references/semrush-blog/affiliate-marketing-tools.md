# Top 18 Affiliate Marketing Tools to Boost Traffic and Conversions

Affiliate marketing involves promoting a brand’s products or services and receiving a commission based on the leads and/or sales you send to that brand.

In this guide, we’ll explore what tools are needed for all the marketing activities you likely do as an affiliate marketer. So you can:

- Better understand your target market
- Connect with relevant brands offering affiliate commissions
- Create engaging affiliate content
- Run an affiliate site successfully

Let’s begin.

## 1. Semrush .Trends

[Semrush’s .Trends](https://www.semrush.com/trends/) solution is a set of four market research tools you can use to learn more about your industry and the brands you want to promote. So you can create effective [affiliate marketing](https://www.semrush.com/blog/affiliate-marketing/) content.

[Market Explorer](https://www.semrush.com/market-explorer/) provides data on specific industries, including their:

- Size
- Geographical distribution
- Audience

Say you want to build an affiliate site focused on solo travel. So you research the “Travel & Tourism” category—like this:

Total addressable market (TAM) refers to the demand from the total number of people in the market.

If the TAM seems small, it may not be a viable market for your affiliate efforts. And not worth pursuing.

[One2Target](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/one2target/) lets you analyze your target audience based on competitors. And you can use it to learn about the audience’s characteristics, so you can create content that appeals to them.

Let’s say One2Target indicates that a particular brand’s audience tends to be primarily females between the ages of 25 and 34. And that YouTube is their favorite social platform.

Knowing this, you could create videos offering tips for female travelers. And distribute them on YouTube.

[Traffic Analytics](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/traffic/) lets you discover how other affiliate marketers attract visitors—and potentially replicate their success.

Let’s say the tool tells you that:

- The majority of a competitor’s users discover it via
[organic search](https://www.semrush.com/blog/organic-content/) - One of this competitor’s top pages is a blog post on a certain topic

You could aim to attract these users by focusing on your [search engine optimization (SEO)](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-is-seo/) efforts. And publishing a more comprehensive blog post on that same topic.

[EyeOn](https://www.semrush.com/eyeon/) tracks websites’ online marketing activities. Like their latest blog and social media posts.

You can use this tool to monitor your partner brands’ latest products and offers. So you can promote them.

Or, use it to keep tabs on your competitors’ activities. To follow what they’re doing and identify marketing opportunities.

#### Key Features

- Understand partner brands’ and competitors’ audience demographics
- Conduct useful industry and market research to inform your affiliate strategy
- Monitor partner brands’ and competitors’ online marketing activities

#### Pricing

**Free plan**: 10 reports a day**Paid plans**: From $289/month for those with an existing Semrush subscription

## 2. Keyword Magic Tool

[Keyword Magic Tool](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/keywordmagic/) helps you discover high-potential search terms to focus your affiliate site’s content on. To increase the chances that your site will appear prominently in search results and attract clicks.

For example, if you run an affiliate site for solo travelers, you could provide Keyword Magic Tool with:

- “solo travel”—as your
[seed keyword](https://www.semrush.com/blog/seed-keywords/) - Your domain—to get data specific to your site

It’ll return a list of related keywords with details like:

**Intent**: The keyword’s[search intent](https://www.semrush.com/blog/search-intent/)—the user’s purpose for entering the query. Knowing this helps you tailor your content to match what people search for.**Volume**: The average number of monthly searches for that keyword. Generally, the higher a keyword’s volume, the more traffic your site may get from it. But keep in mind that higher-volume keywords may be harder to rank for if many others also target them.**Personal Keyword Difficulty (PKD %)**: The tool’s AI-powered score of how easy it may be for your site to rank in the top 10 results for that keyword. Targeting keywords with lower scores (e.g., 0% to 30%) may lead to more and better rankings.

Use this data to shortlist the keywords you want to target. While also considering whether you can mention affiliate offers in the content you create for these keywords.

#### Key Features

- Get keyword ideas to consider for your written content
- Filter keyword results by volume, keyword difficulty, and other metrics. To find keywords that meet your criteria.
- Save keywords to a list for easy access

#### Pricing

**Free plan**: Up to 10 keyword searches per day**Paid plans**: From $139.95/month

## 3. ClickBank

[ClickBank](https://www.clickbank.com/) is one of the most well-known affiliate marketplaces for [earning money as an affiliate marketer](https://www.semrush.com/blog/earn-money-affiliate-marketer/).

Once you’ve found a product you want to promote, copy its affiliate link. Then, add this link to the pages where you talk about that product.

You may earn a commission if users click your affiliate link to buy the product.

#### Key Features

- Get affiliate links for the products you promote
- View your affiliate links’ performance
- Receive payments for confirmed referrals

#### Pricing

- Creating an affiliate account is free
- There’s a $2.50 pay period processing fee for every affiliate payment ClickBank issues to you

## 8. Pretty Links

[Pretty Links](https://prettylinks.com/) is a WordPress plugin that helps you rebrand and manage your affiliate links.

This lets you create custom affiliate links with your branding (most links reflect the brand or affiliate marketplace).

For example, you could create something like this: “yourwebsite.com/go/brand”

Pretty Links then redirects those who use your custom links to the original ones. And keeps count of the number of link clicks.

**Image Source**: Pretty Links

Pretty Links stores all your custom links in a link manager.

If a partner brand’s affiliate link changes, simply update its corresponding custom link in the affiliate marketing tool. Your custom link will automatically redirect to the new affiliate link in all places you’ve used it.

This saves you the trouble of hunting down all uses of the affiliate link on your site. And updating them one by one.

#### Key Features

- Create custom affiliate links
- Track affiliate link clicks
- Manage custom links in one place

#### Pricing

**Free plan**: Unlimited link redirects and link management**Paid plans**: From $249/year (not including a welcome discount) for features like conversion reports and Google Analytics integration

## 10. Backlinks

[Backlinks](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/backlinks/) helps you check a website’s [backlinks](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-are-backlinks/) (links from other websites that lead to it). To find domains that link to competing affiliate sites who you can reach out to for your own backlink.

The more backlinks from reputable sources your site has, the higher it may rank on [search engine results pages (SERPs)](https://www.semrush.com/blog/serp/).

More backlinks also mean more opportunities for users to discover—and visit—your site.

The result?

Your site potentially gets more traffic.

To use the tool, feed it a competing website’s URL. And it’ll reveal information about its backlink profile, including:

- How many backlinks the site has
- Each backlink’s source page
- The
[Authority Score](https://www.semrush.com/blog/semrush-authority-score-explained/)of the backlink’s source page. In general, higher is better.

From there, shortlist the source websites you want backlinks from. And take [steps to get backlinks](https://www.semrush.com/blog/how-to-get-backlinks/) by contacting them and asking for a link.

#### Key Features

- Check any website’s backlinks
- Filter backlinks by various criteria—like whether they’re active or lost
- Compare a website’s backlink profile against up to four others

#### Pricing

**Free plan**: Up to 10 requests per day**Paid plans**: From $139.95/month
