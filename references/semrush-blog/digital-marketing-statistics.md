[Semrush](https://go.semrush.com/Ranking-Factors.html), 2024)

- Featured snippets appear in approximately 4.5% of all top 20 search results on desktop. And about 3.6% on mobile. (
[Semrush Sensor](https://www.semrush.com/sensor), 2024) - More than 92% of domains that rank in the top 100 have at least one backlink (
[Semrush](https://www.semrush.com/blog/how-long-does-it-take-to-rank/), 2023) - Over 50% of qualified sites without backlinks never reach the first page (
[Semrush](https://www.semrush.com/blog/how-long-does-it-take-to-rank/), 2023) - It takes an average of about three months to see backlinks’ impact on search rankings (
[Authority Hacker](https://www.authorityhacker.com/link-building-survey/), 2024) - Titles with positive sentiment have a 4.1% higher CTR than those with negative sentiment (
[Backlinko](https://backlinko.com/google-ctr-stats), 2023) - URLs that include keywords similar to users’ searches have a 45% higher CTR than URLs that don't have similar keywords (
[Backlinko](https://backlinko.com/google-ctr-stats), 2023) - Around 65% of businesses report an improvement in SEO performance when using AI-generated content (
[Semrush](https://www.semrush.com/goodcontent/ai-content-marketing-report/), 2024)
