# How to Promote Your Website (11 Proven SEO & AI Strategies)

Promoting a website today means competing for attention across search engines, social platforms, and even AI-driven tools like Google AI Overviews and ChatGPT.

The good news? Many of the most effective promotion strategies are free or low-cost, and you can start using them right away.

This guide covers 11 proven tactics for boosting your visibility. From technical SEO and AI optimization to social media, communities, and email marketing, you’ll learn how to get more people discovering (and revisiting) your site.

## 1. Optimize for Technical SEO

[Technical SEO](https://www.semrush.com/blog/technical-seo/) is the practice of improving your website so search engines can crawl, index, and rank your pages. Strong technical SEO ensures your content can appear in organic (unpaid) results.

Key tasks include:

- Submitting an
[XML sitemap](https://www.semrush.com/blog/xml-sitemap/)to Google so the right pages are indexed - Building a clear site architecture that shows how pages connect
- Finding and fixing
[broken links](https://www.semrush.com/blog/broken-link/) - Improving page speed, which Google uses in its ranking algorithms

Use Google Search Console for some of these actions. Like submitting your sitemap to Google and checking speed-related metrics via [Core Web Vitals](https://www.semrush.com/blog/core-web-vitals/).

Next, get a free [website audit](https://www.semrush.com/siteaudit/) to surface common technical and AI visibility issues. For deeper coverage across every page, use Semrush Site Audit tool.

After the audit finishes, click on the “**Issues**” tab.

The table is organized into three levels:

**Errors**: High-severity. Fix these quickly because they can impact AI and[SEO visibility](https://www.semrush.com/blog/seo-visibility/).**Warnings**: Medium-severity. Fix after errors.**Notices**: Low-severity. Still useful to address.

If you don’t understand an issue, click “**Why and how to fix it**” for details.

Run audits weekly or monthly. This helps you fix problems before they hurt your site’s visibility.

## 2. Optimize Your Content for AI Models

Optimizing your content for [AI models](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-models/) (like ChatGPT) increases the chance they will mention your brand or cite your website in answers.

For example, in Google [AI Overviews](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-overviews/), AI may pull passages directly from your site:

Unlike search engines, which retrieve full pages, AI models focus on passages that answer questions.

To optimize for passage-level retrieval:

- Publish consistent
[high-quality content](https://www.semrush.com/blog/quality-content/)on relevant topics - Keep each passage focused on one topic or subtopic
- Use subheadings to group related passages in a logical flow

Track your AI visibility with [Position Tracking](https://www.semrush.com/position-tracking/). When setting up a campaign, select either “ChatGPT” or “Google AI Mode.”

## 3. Post on Social Media

Posting on [social media platforms](https://www.semrush.com/blog/most-popular-social-media-platforms/) is free (unless you run ads) and connects you with a wide audience.

[calls to action (CTAs)](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-is-a-call-to-action/)like “Browse sale items” or “Earn 10% off”

## 4. Participate in Online Communities

Online communities like forums and groups are good places to share expertise and answer questions. Providing helpful insights can promote your site naturally.

Some communities also influence AI. In fact, a 2025 [Semrush study](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-mode-comparison-study/) found that Reddit had a 40.11% citation frequency across four major LLMs.

Having a strong presence in these spaces can increase both [AI visibility](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-visibility/) and site promotion.

Start by joining groups relevant to your niche (e.g., on Reddit, Slack, and Facebook).

Then, be active by:

- Sharing valuable resources and original insights
- Answering
[common questions](https://www.semrush.com/kb/1047-find-questions-people-ask)from members - Engaging with posts through comments, likes, and shares

For example, when a user in this group asks for advice on important business trends, group members reply with their perspectives.

You may recommend your products or services when it fits the discussion. But avoid being over-promotional.

Focus on delivering consistent value. If group members find your contributions useful, they may explore your website and offerings on their own.

## 6. Add Your Website to Online Directories

Adding your business to [online directories](https://www.semrush.com/blog/local-business-listing/) can bring referral traffic and improve local search visibility.

Your listings should always include:

- Phone number
- Address
- Hours of operation

This ensures users have accurate details and helps search engines verify your business information.

But not every directory is useful. Focus on high-value platforms like [Google Business Profile](https://www.semrush.com/blog/google-my-business/) (GPB). A complete profile can place your business in the [local pack](https://www.semrush.com/blog/google-local-pack/)— the map feature that highlights businesses in a given area.

Next, add your site to other trusted directories in your industry (e.g., Yelp, Angi, etc.).

To keep your listings effective:

- Update content regularly (e.g., images, holiday hours)
[Respond to customer reviews](https://www.semrush.com/blog/how-to-reply-to-google-reviews/)- Check for consistent information across all platforms to increase your
[local SEO](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-is-local-seo/)potential

To save time, use Semrush’s [Listing Management](https://www.semrush.com/listings-management/) tool. It automatically distributes your business data across top directories.

Once listings are live, they appear in your dashboard. To make changes, click the three vertical dots and click “**Edit info**.”

Accurate listings support local SEO and increase your chances of appearing in AI Overviews and conversational AI answers.

## 7. Publish High-Quality Content on Your Blog

Publishing [blog content](https://www.semrush.com/blog/how-to-write-a-blog-post/) builds authority and may help your site rank for relevant topics. It also increases the chance of being cited in AI-generated answers.

To create blog posts that are likely to gain visibility:

- Perform
[keyword research](https://www.semrush.com/blog/keyword-research/)to find the exact terms your target audience is searching for - Write accurate, useful, and
[high-quality content](https://www.semrush.com/blog/quality-content/) - Structure content for
[NLP](https://www.semrush.com/blog/nlp-seo/)and AI chunk-level retrieval (short, focused passages) - Apply
[on-page SEO](https://www.semrush.com/blog/on-page-seo/)techniques like including internal links and optimizing your title tags to increase the organic ranking potential of your posts

You can find topic ideas for free using [Google’s Keyword Planner](https://ads.google.com/intl/en_us/home/tools/keyword-planner/). Enter a topic to see related terms, search volume, and trend data.

For AI-focused insights, use Semrush’s[ AI SEOToolkit](https://www.semrush.com/ai-seo/brand-performance/). In the “**Questions**” report you’ll see which themes and queries appear most often in AI platforms.

Use these insights to:

- Identify high-interest topics your audience cares about
- Align content with the
[search intent](https://www.semrush.com/blog/search-intent/)behind queries - Spot content gaps your competitors haven’t covered

## 8. Create Shareable Resources

Creating sharable resources encourages others to promote your website. When someone shares your content on social media, their audience may visit your site to explore more. This leads to additional [website traffic](https://www.semrush.com/blog/website-traffic/) and visibility.

Examples of shareable resources include:

**Webinars**: Online workshops or presentations on industry topics**Reports**: Data-driven resources that highlight trends: Examples of successful customer relationships**Case studies****Podcast episodes**: Downloadable or streamable discussions on industry topics: Visuals combining text and graphics to explain data**Infographics****Free tools**: Practical tools like calculators or AI generators

Reports, case studies, and original data are especially powerful. LLMs frequently cite them, which increases your chances of being referenced in AI answers. Publishing unique insights expands your reach beyond traditional search engines.

## 9. Use Email Marketing

[Email marketing](https://www.semrush.com/blog/email-marketing/) lets you reach users who already showed interest by signing up for updates. It helps bring them back to your website.

Many brands do this with regular newsletters. For example, Exploding Topics sends curated email updates:

To use email marketing effectively:

- Choose an
[email marketing platform](https://www.semrush.com/blog/email-marketing-platforms/)to send and track campaigns - Build a subscriber list with
[lead magnets](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-is-lead-magnet/)and signup forms - Create different email types (e.g., promotions, discounts, new content)
- Send emails regularly but avoid overwhelming subscribers, which can cause them to unsubscribe

Email marketing data can also improve AI optimization. Subject lines or CTAs with high engagement can be repurposed into AI-friendly headings and passages. This makes your content more likely to be retrieved by large language models.

Email marketing is also low-cost and beginner-friendly. You can start for free with platforms like MailChimp.

## 10. Work with Influencers

[Influencer marketing](https://www.semrush.com/blog/influencer-marketing-guide/) gives you access to the audiences of people with loyal followings, usually on social media.

Influencers can promote your site by mentioning your brand—and often by linking to it.

For example, Subway partnered with this influencer to promote a new product.

To find influencers in your niche, use [Influencer Analytics](https://www.semrush.com/apps/influencer-marketing-platform/).

In the “**Influencer Discovery**” tab, browse potential partners and filter results based on location, language, audience size, and more.

When you find someone a good fit:

- Contact the influencer to confirm interest
- Propose content that supports your goals (e.g., one post per month, five posts in total, etc.)
- Create a contract that defines the agreement (e.g., start and end date, branding requirements, payment terms, etc.)

After the influencer posts, track results such as traffic, conversions, or sales. Use tracking parameters in campaign links to measure impact.

This data shows whether the partnership was successful and if you should continue working with that influencer.

## 11. Use Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising

[Pay-per-click](https://www.semrush.com/blog/what-is-ppc/) (PPC) advertising is a paid method to promote your website. You pay each time someone clicks on your ad.

You can run [PPC campaigns](https://www.semrush.com/blog/how-to-plan-ppc-campaign/) on:

- Search engines like Google and Bing
- Social media platforms like Facebook and X

[Google Display Network](https://www.semrush.com/blog/google-display-network/)) - YouTube Ads
