# How do you evaluate ROI from AI optimization software?

You evaluate ROI from [AI optimization software](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-optimization-tools/) by establishing baseline metrics before implementation, tracking all costs, and measuring business improvements over time.

Start by defining specific goals like revenue increase or efficiency gains. Then calculate the benefits against your total costs to determine your return on investment.

Here's how to measure AI optimization ROI effectively:

**Establish clear baselines before implementation.**Record current performance metrics like conversion rates or content production time so you can measure actual improvement**Track all costs including hidden expenses.**Include software fees, training time, and ongoing management hours for your true total investment**Measure business improvements.**Focus on revenue increases, cost reductions, or efficiency gains that directly impact your bottom line, not just vanity metrics**Set specific timeframes for evaluation.**Measure ROI at 30, 90, and 180 days since AI tools often improve over time as the system learns**Monitor key performance indicators continuously.**Track campaign performance, content quality, and time saved on manual tasks to ensure ROI maintains or improves
