# What is AI marketing?

AI marketing is the use of artificial intelligence technologies to automate, personalize, and optimize your marketing processes and campaigns using machine learning and data analysis.

Instead of manually segmenting audiences or guessing what content will perform best, [AI marketing](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-marketing/) tools analyze customer data to make those decisions for you. This helps you deliver more relevant experiences while spending less time on repetitive tasks.

Here's how AI transforms marketing:

**Automate repetitive tasks.**AI handles email scheduling, social media posting, and ad bidding so you can focus on strategy and creative work**Personalize customer experiences**. Machine learning analyzes user behavior to show different website content, product recommendations, or email campaigns to each visitor**Predict customer behavior.**AI identifies which leads are most likely to convert, which customers might churn, and what products to recommend next**Optimize campaign performance.**Algorithms automatically adjust ad targeting, bidding, and creative elements to improve ROI across channels like Google Ads and Facebook**Generate and test content.**AI creates multiple versions of ad copy, email subject lines, or landing page headlines, then identifies what performs best

For example, instead of sending the same email newsletter to your entire list, AI marketing tools can automatically send different subject lines and content based on each subscriber's past engagement and preferences.
