# YouTube Backlinks: Why They Matter and How to Build Them for SEO

## What Are YouTube Backlinks?

YouTube backlinks are external links that direct users from a YouTube video or its associated content to your website.

For example, a YouTube channel may include a link to a related blog post in its video description.

Building backlinks from reputable sources is an essential part of any [off-page search engine optimization (SEO)](https://www.semrush.com/blog/off-page-seo/) strategy. YouTube backlinks can increase search rankings and drive more organic traffic to your website.

### How Does a Backlink Work?

When a reputable website links to another website, search engines view the linked site as a trustworthy source of information. Backlinks serve as votes of confidence, and search engines use them to determine organic search rankings.

### What Makes YouTube Backlinks Reputable?

YouTube has an Authority Score of 100, according to [Semrush’s Authority Score](https://www.semrush.com/kb/747-authority-score-backlink-scores#:~:text=Authority%20Score%20is%20our%20compound,quality%20and%20quantity%20of%20backlinks) metric. This metric rates domain authority on a scale from 0 to 100. A higher authority score indicates more valuable outbound links.

Because YouTube’s domain authority is at the top of the scale, a backlink from YouTube can offer significant value.

## Why Use YouTube as Part of Your Backlink Strategy: The Benefits

A backlink strategy (or a [link-building strategy](https://www.semrush.com/blog/link-building/)) refers to the tactics a website uses to secure high-value backlinks.

YouTube backlinking is a strong option for businesses that already produce content for YouTube.

### 1. Enhance Your Search Engine Optimization

A [Backlinko study](https://backlinko.com/search-engine-ranking?_gl=1*1041k0f*_gcl_au*MTIwOTIyMTU4My4xNzMzNDQxNDQz*_ga*MTYxNzM2NjcxNS4xNzMzNDQxNDQz*_ga_BPNLXP3JQG*MTczNDI3NjM4NC42LjEuMTczNDI5NzE2Ny4wLjAuMTYyNTk4NDY4MA..*_ga_HYWKMHR981*MTczNDI3NjM4My42LjEuMTczNDI5NzE2Ny42MC4wLjA.) suggests that high [website authority](https://www.semrush.com/blog/how-to-build-website-authority/#5-tips-on-how-to-increase-website-authority) correlates strongly with higher rankings.

Links from highly authoritative sites can improve your own authority score. YouTube’s domain authority ranks at the top level. Backlinks from this platform can help your site perform better on [search engine results pages (SERPs)](https://www.semrush.com/blog/serp/).

Receiving links from YouTube, as well as social media platforms and other reputable websites, can improve your overall backlink profile. A backlink profile measures the number and quality of domains linking to a website.

A diverse backlink profile signals [credibility and relevance](https://www.google.com/search/howsearchworks/how-search-works/ranking-results/) to search engines. This can boost your website’s organic rankings.

The Backlinko study also shows that backlinks from diverse domains support SEO. If you already operate a YouTube channel, creating links from there requires minimal effort.

Starting with a YouTube backlink is an easy first step to diversify your link profile.

### 5. Increase Your Brand Value and Credibility

A backlink from a well-known or reputable source can increase trust in your business. Many users trust YouTube more than other platforms during their shopping journey.

When users arrive at your website from a YouTube backlink, they often associate your brand with a credible source. This can increase positive brand perception, recognition, and long-term value.

For example, DreamHost—a leader in web hosting—links to [LezWatch.TV](http://lezwatch.tv) in its YouTube video descriptions. This strategy not only drives traffic but also transfers some of DreamHost’s credibility to LezWatch.TV.

### 6. Diversify Your Marketing

Linking from your YouTube videos to your website helps build a cross-platform audience. [Google reports](https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/consumer-insights/consumer-trends/meeting-shoppers-needs-omnichannel-drives-instore/) that an [omnichannel strategy](https://www.semrush.com/blog/omnichannel-marketing/) can increase incremental store visits by up to 80% for e-commerce brands.

Omnichannel marketing integrates multiple channels to offer a unified customer experience. Moving viewers seamlessly from YouTube videos to your website can have a direct positive impact on site traffic.

Cross-linking also protects against platform-specific setbacks. For example, [Vine shut down in 2017](https://money.cnn.com/2017/01/17/technology/vine-shuts-down/index.html). Many [creators directed their Vine followers to YouTube](https://www.forbes.com/sites/masonsands/2019/01/17/two-years-later-where-is-the-viner-invasion/?sh=223221c52b8b), ensuring they maintained their audience elsewhere.

Similarly, if a [Google algorithm update](https://developers.google.com/search/updates/core-updates) affects your site’s rankings, YouTube backlinks can still drive referral traffic. According to Statista, YouTube backlinks drive [24%](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1257502/social-referrals-to-ecommerce-websites-by-traffic/) of all e-commerce referral traffic—second only to Facebook.

This traffic can help preserve your brand’s equity if organic search traffic declines.

For more on recovering from Google Updates, see: [How to Survive Google Updates: A Step-by-Step Guide](https://www.semrush.com/blog/google-algorithm-update/).

### 7. It’s Easier Than Other Types of Backlinks

Securing backlinks from high-authority websites (with scores of 60-100) often proves challenging. However, you can leverage your existing YouTube videos to create these valuable links easily.

Many SEO specialists spend between [$5,000 and $10,000 per year](https://userp.io/state-of-backlinks-for-seo/) on link building. YouTube backlinks are often more cost-effective because you can use existing content without extra production costs.

You also control where and when you link out from your own YouTube content. Other methods, such as guest posting, may limit your linking options due to host site guidelines.

In summary: YouTube backlinks can improve SEO, drive leads and sales, reach new audiences, build brand awareness, increase brand value, diversify marketing strategies, and provide an easier method for gaining high-authority links.

## How to Build Different Types of YouTube Backlinks

Your YouTube channel provides several ways to direct viewers to your business website. You can categorize YouTube backlinks based on their placement on the platform.

### Video Description Backlinks

Video description links stand out because they appear directly below the video. They’re easy to create by simply including the URL in the description field when uploading the video.

Link to your homepage or another relevant page, but ensure your video content relates to the linked webpage so viewers understand the connection.

### Profile Backlinks

YouTube profile links appear below your channel’s banner.

You can add up to 14 profile links. Many brands link to Instagram, Facebook, or other platforms.

Use YouTube Studio to add these links. After logging in, click your avatar in the top right corner and select “**YouTube Studio**.”

In the left-side navigation, choose “**Customization**” and then “**Basic info**.”

Click “**+ ADD LINK**,” name your link, and paste the URL.

### Card Links

YouTube cards let you add interactive links during the video. These links usually point to other YouTube videos or playlists, but if you belong to the YouTube Partner Program (YPP), you can also add external links.

You can place up to five cards per video, guiding viewers toward specific website pages.

**To add a card link in YouTube Studio**:

- Log in to YouTube Studio and select “
**Content**” in the left menu - Choose a video by clicking its title or thumbnail
- Click “
**Editor**” from the left menu - Go to “Info cards” and select “
**Link**” - Add the desired external URL

### Comment Backlinks

Comment links can drive users to specific pages on your website. Some creators also use them to add affiliate links.

Use comment links sparingly. Overuse may trigger spam alerts and lead to account suspension. For more information on allowed comments, refer to the [Google Support page](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/2801973?hl=en#zippy=%2Ccomments-spam).

### End-Screen Backlinks

End screens appear at the end of videos and often show suggested content. You can use them to guide viewers to your website. End-screen backlinks serve as [calls to action (CTAs)](https://www.semrush.com/blog/call-to-action-examples/) that encourage viewers to take a next step.

If you are a YPP creator, you can add external links to end screens to increase referral traffic.

**To add an end-screen link**:

- Follow steps one and two from the card link instructions above
- Click “
**Editor**” from the left menu - Select “
**End screen**” - Choose “
**Link**” and add your webpage URL

Don’t use free YouTube backlink generators. These tools create unnatural links and can result in account suspension.
