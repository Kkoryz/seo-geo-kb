# What is keyword analysis?

Keyword analysis is the process of identifying and examining the words and phrases people enter into search engines when looking for information, products, or services online. By understanding how your audience searches, you can create content and marketing campaigns that speak directly to their needs.

Keyword analysis can show what people are looking for, how often they search for it, how they phrase their searches, and how difficult it might be to rank for certain terms. It also reveals why people search in a particular way, which helps you develop a more focused and effective content strategy. These insights allow you to find the best opportunities to attract visitors who are more likely to become customers or subscribers.

Once you know which keywords work well, you can refine existing content, create new pages that address popular questions, and adjust your ads or other content to reach the right people. By focusing on the terms your audience uses, your site becomes more visible in search results and can attract better outcomes over time.
