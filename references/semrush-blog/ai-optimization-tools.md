## What Is an AI Optimization Tool?

An AI optimization tool is a piece of software that shows you how large language models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity mention your brand in their answers and helps you improve your presence.

One great example is the [Semrush](https://semrush.com/). It tracks your presence across all the major AI platforms.

AI optimization tools query various LLMs with relevant prompts and track the results. You can see whether your brand, product, or service gets mentioned, how it's described, and in what context.

This gives you an idea about your AI presence so you can take actions to improve.

These tools have become essential because a lot of people are relying on AI to find information online.

ChatGPT [reportedly](https://www.forbes.com/sites/martineparis/2025/04/12/chatgpt-hits-1-billion-users-openai-ceo-says-doubled-in-weeks/) has around 1 billion active users. Google’s AI Overviews now appear in [billions of searches](https://abc.xyz/assets/34/fa/ee06f3de4338b99acffc5c229d9f/2025q1-alphabet-earnings-release.pdf) each month, making AI-generated results just as important—if not more so—than traditional blue links.

In other words: If your brand isn’t showing up in AI answers, you’re missing out on a huge share of visibility and potential traffic.

### Common Terms You'll See

The goal of AI optimization tools is to help you get better visibility in AI platforms.

The process in which you use these tools is AI optimization—but you’ll see a few other names for it too:

- GEO (generative engine optimization)
- AEO (answer engine optimization)
- LLM optimization
- AI search optimization

They all basically mean the same thing: boosting your brand’s visibility in tools powered by LLMs.

AI visibility is often a separate playing field from traditional SEO.
