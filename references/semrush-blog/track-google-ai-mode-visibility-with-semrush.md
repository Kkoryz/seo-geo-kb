# How to Track Your Google AI Mode Visibility with Semrush

Google’s AI Mode is a search experience that uses generative AI to answer queries directly in Google Search.

By showing up there, your content can gain visibility, position your brand as a thought leader, and drive clicks.

However, Google currently provides only a limited view of AI Mode performance, leaving room to better understand your visibility and uncover opportunities to grow.

Semrush’s AI Visibility Toolkit (formerly known as the AI SEO Toolkit) fills that gap. In this guide, you’ll learn how to use it to track your AI Mode visibility step by step.

## 1. Analyze Your Brand’s Current Visibility in AI Mode

Start by analyzing where your brand currently appears in AI Mode answers and how you compare to other players in your niche.

To do this, open the [Visibility Overview report](https://www.semrush.com/ai-seo/overview/) in the Semrush AI Visibility Toolkit and enter your domain.

Select "**AI Mode**" from the platform filter at the top.

From here, you’ll see the monthly trend of how often your brand is mentioned in AI Mode answers (Mentions), and how many pages from your site are cited by AI Mode (Cited Pages).

The **Topics & Sources** section helps you understand where your brand is showing up in AI answers—and where it’s missing.

**Your Performing Topics:**topics where your brand is already mentioned. These show what’s working.**Topic Opportunities:**topics where your competitors are visible, but your brand isn’t—revealing clear content gaps to close.**Cited Sources:**websites AI tools reference when generating answers. These shape how your brand is represented.**Source Opportunities:**commonly cited sites that feature your competitors but not you. Getting mentioned there could boost your AI visibility.**Cited Pages:**your company’s own websites or content (like your blog or help center) that AI cited as a source in its responses.

Pay special attention to Cited Pages. They show what parts of your content strategy are already influencing AI, helping you double down on what’s driving results. Run this report on a competitor’s domain to measure how their content strategy wins visibility in AI Mode.

In this section, you’ll find a table that compares your brand’s AI visibility to your competitors across different topics and prompts:

These insights help you identify where you’re leading, where you’re falling behind, and where you’re missing entirely—giving you a clear roadmap to close visibility gaps and boost your presence in AI-generated answers.

## 3. Analyze Your Brand’s Sentiment in AI Mode

Next, look at how AI Mode describes your brand.

This shows you any gaps between how AI tools see you versus how you want to be seen. Use these insights to improve your messaging and boost your visibility in AI responses.

In the AI Visibility Toolkit, head to the “**Perception**” report.

Start by analyzing the “**Competitive Perception**” chart to see how your brand's sentiment compares to competitors in Google AI Mode (you can also check this across other platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity).

If you notice certain platforms consistently favor your competitors, check out their messaging strategies to understand what's working for them.

Then, review the “**Key Sentiment Drivers**” section to understand what aspects of your brand AI tools tend to highlight positively and negatively.

Use the brand strength factors to reinforce your marketing messages and product offerings. Then look at the areas for improvement to identify opportunities to turn negative sentiment into positive.

4. Apply Strategic Opportunities for Your Business

You can also use the data from your AI Mode analysis as a strategic feedback loop for your entire business. This helps you spot messaging gaps, product opportunities, and competitive advantages.

The Brand Performance reports provide these insights through the “AI Strategic Opportunities” section at the bottom of each report.

For example, you can find tips for offering new products, launching campaigns, tweaking your messaging, and more.

Plus, each opportunity comes with concrete action steps and priority levels to help with planning.

## 5. Track Your Visibility in Google AI Mode

Finally, start tracking your Google AI Mode visibility for your key terms. This shows you how your new and optimized content is performing for your target keywords and prompts.

Head to Semrush’s [Position Tracking tool](https://www.semrush.com/position-tracking/) and create a new project. Select “**Google AI Mode**” as the search engine. (You can also track your rankings in ChatGPT and traditional search engines.)

Next, add the keywords you want to track in Google AI Mode. You can add prompts manually, from a file, or from Semrush’s prompt suggestions.

Once you have your list of prompts to track, click “**Start Tracking**.”

In the Position Tracking dashboard, you’ll see your brand’s AI visibility, mentions and average AI Mode position across your tracked prompts.

For every keyword you track, the tool shows whether your brand name is mentioned in the response, along with your number of Owned Sources (pages on your site being cited), position of your cited page, and overall AI visibility score.

In addition, you can track these rankings against your competitors. Just add a specific domain to compare against or choose one from the tool’s suggested options.

## FAQs About Google AI Mode

### How Does Google’s AI Mode Work?

Google's AI Mode uses Google’s latest Gemini model to generate direct answers to search queries, instead of showing traditional search results.

When you submit a question to AI Mode, it:

- Breaks your query into multiple sub-questions (this process is known as
[query fan-out](https://www.semrush.com/blog/query-fan-out/)) - Retrieves relevant content from across the web for each part
- Builds a comprehensive response that synthesizes this information
- Cites original sources with links

### How Do I Grow My Visibility in AI Mode?

To grow your visibility in Google's AI Mode, you need a multi-level approach focused on brand authority, content strategy, and platform diversity. This involves optimizing your website and content while also building your reputation across the web.

In a [Semrush study](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-mode-comparison-study/) analyzing 5,000 keywords and 150,000 citations across AI platforms, we discovered three key strategies to improve your AI Mode visibility:

**Strengthen your brand's overall authority**: Focus on creating quality content throughout your site, not just your main landing pages. Since AI Mode pulls from different pages than traditional search (only 35% URL overlap vs 67% for[AI Overviews](https://www.semrush.com/blog/semrush-ai-overview-research/)), improving content across your entire site increases your chances of being cited.

**Match content depth to query intent**: Keep informational content concise and direct. But create in-depth comparisons and detailed guides for commercial intent keywords to maximize your citation chances. The study showed that AI Mode’s responses to commercial queries are nearly twice as long as those to informational ones.

**Expand beyond your website**: Develop content for other platforms like YouTube and Facebook, which receive more AI Mode citations than many traditional websites. Also establish yourself on Reddit, as it appears in 68% of AI Mode answers that contain external links.
