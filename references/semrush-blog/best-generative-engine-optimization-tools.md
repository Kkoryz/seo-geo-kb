**Actionable visibility insights**: Sentiment analysis, share of voice, competitor benchmarking, and missed opportunities surfaced in plain language

**Optimization workflows**: Built-in recommendations, experiments, or playbooks to improve AI visibility and drive broader business outcomes

**Competitor benchmarking**: Side-by-side visibility and sentiment comparisons to see where you lead or lag in your category
