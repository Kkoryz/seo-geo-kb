This AI-powered tool is not just smart; it’s specialized for your website.

Unlike generic forecasting tools, Semrush uses domain-specific metrics to calculate its estimates. It analyzes keywords in the context of your domain's highest possible position, factoring in your competitive landscape and topical authority.

This tailored approach results in more accurate, relevant predictions for your specific website.

This approach leads to more reliable forecasts and, ultimately, better-informed SEO decisions.

## Where to Find Traffic Predictions in Semrush

Semrush has added Potential Traffic as a metric to its Keyword Overview, Keyword Magic Tool, and Position Tracking tool. Here’s where you’ll see it in each.

### Keyword Overview

To begin, enter your keywords and domain into [Keyword Overview](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/keywordoverview/?db=us).

Select your country and click “**Search**.”

The tool will return the national and global keyword volume along with personalized data, like the Personalized Keyword Difficulty, the domain’s Topical Authority, and the potential SERP position.

*This estimate is calculated using several key factors: your domain's Authority Score, Personal Keyword Difficulty (PKD%), potential ranking position, expected traffic volumes, and the competitive landscape on the SERP.*

To see the list of keywords that make up this topic, click on “See topic details” to open the Bulk Analysis.

Here you’ll get a breakdown of the entire topic, and how much potential traffic your site could get if you successfully targeted every keyword with SEO.

If you already have a list of keywords you want to forecast potential traffic for, you can check them in the Bulk Analysis section. Simply enter your keyword list and click “**Analyze**” to review the traffic prediction for the provided keywords.

The tool details the potential traffic gain alongside the Personal Keyword Difficulty score for your domain, giving you insights on which keywords would be easiest for your domain to compete for first.

### Keyword Magic Tool

Navigate to the [Keyword Magic Tool](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/keywordmagic/start) and enter your seed keyword, domain, and target country. Then click “**Search**.”

The tool now displays a "Potential traffic" column for each keyword alongside the [Personal Keyword Difficulty](https://www.semrush.com/kb/1434-how-is-personal-keyword-difficulty-calculated).

This metric shows the estimated monthly traffic your domain could receive if ranking in the top potential position estimated by Semrush.

To get the most out of this feature, sort your results by "Potential traffic" to spot keywords that are most relevant to your site and could drive significant traffic.

### Position Tracking

By referencing this feature in the [Position Tracking](https://www.semrush.com/position-tracking/) tool, you can gain deeper insights into how ranking for all of your target keywords would impact the site’s overall traffic.

Based on the target keywords added to a Position Tracking campaign, the tool now offers a 3-month traffic potential prediction for your reference.

For instance, let’s imagine you’re managing SEO for goodbookstore.com. You've been monitoring your keyword positions, but your boss wants to know how much growth can be expected.

Traffic Predictions within Position Tracking can show you exactly that.

**How to Use It:** To access Traffic Predictions, go to the [Position Tracking](https://www.semrush.com/position-tracking/) tool and launch a new campaign with your target keywords.

Here’s a breakdown of what the tool will show in the Position Tracking Landscape view:

Notice the current stats vs. potential:

- Visibility: Currently at 3.12%, with potential to increase by 6.10%
- Estimated Traffic: Currently at 83.76 daily visits, with room to grow by 214.3
- Average Position: Currently at 45.96, with potential to climb 10.45 positions

These forecasts show the potential improvements you could see for your specified list of target keywords if you engage in similar optimization efforts.

*To determine these estimates, the Position Tracking tool uses a prediction model with data from our trusted agencies that focus on content optimization and improving domains. *

In the **Overview tab**, you'll find a table that provides forecasts for potential traffic and growth for specific keywords.

By sorting this table by "**Your Potential Growth**," you can find the keywords that offer the greatest opportunities for improvement.

**Adjusting Tracking Parameters**

The Traffic Prediction feature in Position Tracking uses the parameters you set during your Project setup. However, you can easily adjust these settings.

To change parameters regarding your website tracking, like location or search engine, simply click the current settings under the Position Tracking title and click “**+ Add new target**.”

This allows you to update tracking details such as location or search engine.

By customizing these settings, you ensure that the Traffic Prediction feature provides data that's most relevant to your specific SEO goals.
