# How to Use AI for a Content Strategy That Drives Results

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has the potential to change your content marketing by streamlining tasks and processes that traditionally take a lot of time and energy.

And incorporating AI tools into your work is easier than you might expect.

By the time you’re done reading this article, you’ll understand exactly how you can start using AI throughout every phase of planning and executing your content strategy.

But let’s first go over why using AI is important in the first place.

## The Benefits of an AI-Driven Content Strategy

The main benefits of using AI when creating and launching your [content strategy](https://www.semrush.com/blog/content-marketing-strategy-guide/) are that it helps you save time and reduce costs.

Specifically, you can benefit from using AI by:

**Automating repetitive tasks**and freeing up time for creative or strategic thinking**Better meeting your audience’s needs**by creating the types of content they’re looking for**Avoiding creative burnout**by using[AI content generators](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-content-generators/)for drafting**Gathering insights from lots of data**without having to manually sift through it**Getting more out of your marketing budget**by increasing your output without having to hire more creators

## How to Use AI to Execute Your Content Strategy

Here are some ways to incorporate AI in your content strategy:

### 1. Speed Up Keyword Research

Using [AI for keyword research](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-keyword-research/) involves using chatbots (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) to quickly find terms that may help your website gain visibility in organic (unpaid) search results and AI search results.

AI chatbots don’t know exactly what users search for in Google. But they can suggest relevant keyword ideas based on the topic you include in your prompt.

For example, I asked ChatGPT to provide keyword ideas related to “dog toys” to write a blog post on. It provided a list of relevant keywords and organized them by [intent](https://www.semrush.com/blog/search-intent/) (what the searcher is trying to achieve).

For research that's based on actual [keyword metrics](https://www.semrush.com/blog/keyword-metrics/), run a free [keyword tool](https://www.semrush.com/analytics/keywordmagic/) for the basics. For AI-powered metrics tailored to your specific site, use Semrush Keyword Magic Tool.

For example, you’ll get a [Personal Keyword Difficulty](https://www.semrush.com/kb/1434-how-is-personal-keyword-difficulty-calculated) (PKD %) score for each keyword. This estimates how difficult it will be for your site to rank on the first page of Google’s results.

To focus on ideas that will work well for a blog post, select the “**Intent**” filter, check the box next to “Informational,” and click “**Apply**.” This will narrow the list to show terms that people search when they want to learn about a topic.

Prioritize the keywords with low PKD % scores and reasonable search volumes. Because that combination gives you a good chance of ranking for terms that are likely to drive people to your website.

To find terms that are similar to the conversational prompts people enter into AI tools, use the “Questions” filter.

Focus on terms that seem like the best fit and add those keywords to your content plans.

### 2. Tailor Your Content to Your Target Audience

Certain AI tools help you refine your [target audience](https://www.semrush.com/blog/target-audience/), which makes it easier to create content that’s likely to resonate with them.

For example, the [AI Marketing Agent](https://www.semrush.com/apps/ai-marketing-agent/) app lets you enter information about an upcoming campaign to generate recommendations based on your business and audience specifics.

Start by entering some details about the campaign you want to create.

The app will ask you follow-up questions as needed to get all the information it needs. You’ll then get a complete strategy and campaign assets—including copy and visuals—that are tailored to your exact audience.

### 4. Cluster Keywords & Topics

[Keyword clustering](https://www.semrush.com/blog/keyword-clustering/) involves targeting a group of keywords with similar search intent and relevance on the same page, and you can do it much faster using AI than you ever could manually.

For example, you can cluster keywords using [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/app). Make sure to mention “keyword clusters” in your prompt and enter all your keywords. Gemini will then group terms for you.

For more guidance on how to write prompts for SEO purposes like this, read our article on [maximizing SEO impact with ChatGPT](https://www.semrush.com/blog/chatgpt-seo/).

### 5. Optimize Content for Search

AI tools can show you exactly what changes to make to your webpages to improve their performance in organic search, which is called [on-page SEO](https://www.semrush.com/blog/on-page-seo/). And these techniques generally help you optimize for appearance in AI search.

On-page SEO includes optimizing the following:

**Title tag**: An HTML title that may appear as a blue, clickable hyperlink in search engine results**Meta description**: An HTML page summary that may appear below the title in search results: HTML elements that define headings and subheadings on a page**Headings****Body content**: The main content on a page like text, images, and videos: A uniform resource locator (URL) is the webpage’s address**URL**

Use Semrush’s [SEO Writing Assistant](https://www.semrush.com/swa/) to confirm that your content is optimized for ranking before you publish it. The tool will check your content for tone of voice, readability, SEO, and originality. And grade your content on a scale out of 10.

You can improve your score by following the suggestions in the right-hand sidebar.

You can also use the SEO Writing Assistant’s Smart Writer features to speed up the writing process. For example, the rephraser tool lets you enter text to rephrase, simplify, expand, or summarize.

After you generate text, click “**Paste and close**” to add it to your content.

### 6. Maintain a Consistent Brand Voice

Your [brand voice](https://www.semrush.com/blog/brand-voice/) can impact how your audience feels about your business, and you can use AI to define, improve, and keep yours consistent.

To define your brand voice, you’ll want to:

- Research your audience to learn about their challenges or preferences and how your brand can meet them
- Document your company’s background and mission, like your purpose and
[unique value proposition](https://www.semrush.com/blog/unique-value-proposition/)(UVP) - Define your brand personality by choosing three to four words to describe your business (e.g., casual, innovative, warm, and informative)

You can then enter information about your brand voice into ChatGPT as part of a prompt when you ask it to create content.

Or use Semrush’s [Content Toolkit](https://www.semrush.com/content/) to speed things up. To set your brand voice inside the Content Toolkit, click the gear icon at the top of the main dashboard and navigate to the “Brand Voice” tab.

Click “**+ Create Brand Voice**.”

Copy and paste some content from your website into the Brand Voice feature (ideally, choose copy that best represents your brand). And click “**Create Brand Voice**”

### 7. Repurpose Content

AI tools can help you quickly [repurpose content](https://www.semrush.com/blog/repurposing-content/), so you can get more from each piece and reach a wider audience.

For example, the [AI Social Content Generator](https://www.semrush.com/apps/ai-social-content-generator/) app lets you generate social posts from content that’s already on your website. In the app, select “Social Media” and choose the type of post you want to create.

Choose “**Text to Post**" the drop-down at the top, briefly summarize your content (depending on the field provided), and make any adjustments with the settings.

Then, let AI Social Content Generator create your posts.

If needed, edit the AI-generated posts to better fit your brand voice or audience preferences. Then, publish them to your social media accounts.

### 8. Generate Content at Scale

AI tools let you create more content faster to help you [scale your content efforts](https://www.semrush.com/blog/15-tips-for-scaling-your-seo-content-strategy/) and possibly grow your brand awareness.

Specifically, scaling your content production helps with:

- Covering more topics on your website to increase your
[topical authority](https://www.semrush.com/blog/topical-authority/)(your expertise)—and possibly your traffic from traditional search and AI search - Appealing to a greater number of audience segments
- Having more content to promote across channels (e.g., blog posts, videos, and
[podcast episodes](https://www.semrush.com/blog/podcast-seo/))

Use Semrush’s [AI Article Generator](https://www.semrush.com/content/articles/create) to generate SEO-friendly blog posts at scale. Get started by entering a topic or idea and clicking “**Get suggestions**.”

Customize your parameters (e.g., title, target keywords, etc.) if needed and make sure you’ve checked the box next to “SEO booster.” And click “**Create article**.”

Starting your articles from a working draft (rather than a blank page) can save you significant time. So you can publish more content that your audience is interested in.
